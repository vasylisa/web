THE TYPES OF COMPUTER GAMES

Computer games are one of the most popular entertainment in the modern world. It is a huge industry, a business where every person of the new generation will find something for themselves. Games are different. People are different. Everyone will find something for themselves.

RPG

Computer Role-Playing Game; a genre of computer games based on the gameplay elements of traditional tabletop role-playing games. The player controls one or more characters, each of which is described by a set of numerical characteristics, a list of abilities and skills; RPG games have become popular because of the atmosphere and immersion in the story quests.There are also subgenres: MMORPG, ACTION RPG. MMORPG is "your second life" - a network game in which you create your own character and go to travel the world.The advantages of such games are"infinity", as every day there are network events and various competitions,PVP and much more.

The Witcher

The Witcher is a fantasy RPG series developed by CD Projekt Red and published by CD Projekt. It is based on the book series of the same name by the Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski, which is a continuation of the history of these books.

The Elder Scrolls

A series of computer role-playing games developed by Bethesda Game Studios and produced by Bethesda Softworks. The games of the series are united by a common, detailed universe and provide the player with greater freedom. Four games in the series - Morrowind, Oblivion, Skyrim, and Online.

Dark Souls

Dark Souls is a new role-playing game from FromSoftware, who created the game Demon's Souls. In Dark Souls, you will encounter many familiar elements: a dark fantasy universe, dark dungeons, battles with terrifying enemies, and unique opportunities to interact with other players.

Fallout

A series of post-apocalyptic computer role-playing games produced by Interplay Entertainment, Black Isle Studios, Micro Forte, Bethesda Softworks, and Obsidian Entertainment. The action takes place after a nuclear war on the territory of the United States, which has turned into a radioactive desert and is engulfed in anarchy.

LOGIC

Logic games are those games in which you need to think a lot.In fact, we have all encountered them at least once in our lives:cards,chess, spider or"balls".The advantage of such games is versatility - age, gender,race-it does not matter,it is important to be able to think logically and analyze the situation.Logical games include "Poker" and similar gambling games.

PILOT BROTHERS

Edward Ouspensky created two brilliant detectives who are able to compete with Sherlock Holmes and Hercule Poirot in their resourcefulness at the same time. Developers from Gamos and Pilot have created a whole series of games where a sense of humor, resourcefulness and creativity help players solve cases.

LIMBO

You go to the first circle of hell, where you always need to move only forward, otherwise the darkness will engulf you. The game is divided into levels, and the passage of the first is quite easy, while the second causes difficulties, and the third and further can be overcome only by the most resourceful gamers. Limbo has won many awards, and gamers from all over the world called the game one of the most interesting puzzles, where you never know what awaits in a couple of seconds.

ACTION

Action is a genre of computer games that focuses on exploiting the player's physical abilities, including eye-hand coordination and reaction speed.

Dishonored

Dishonored is a first-person stealth action game with elements of a role-playing game, made in the style of steampunk. The game takes place in the fictional plague-ridden city of Dunwall, the prototype of which was London during the Victorian era. The main character of the game, Lord protector Corvo Attano, after being accused of murdering the empress and escaping from prison, tries to take revenge on his enemies by joining a rebel group that plans to overthrow the Lord Regent, and give power to Princess Emilia.

The game requires a quick reaction, the dynamics of actions and thinking, so you will not be bored in it and in games of this genre.

SIMULATOR

Simulator-a genre of computer and video games, where the basis of the gameplay is the most detailed simulation of any action. (Driving a car, controlling an airplane or train, etc.) Game simulators were not always such, they were originally created in order to protect and simplify the training of people responsible for managing high-risk vehicles.

Minecraft

An indie computer game in the sandbox genre, originally created by Swedish programmer Markus Persson and released by his company Mojang AB.Minecraft is a computer game with endless worlds created from blocks, where you need to build and survive. The main character of the game is Steve, who is controlled by the player.
